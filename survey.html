Regional Housing Study
Public Survey

The survey below was created by CommunityScale for MACOG, the Michiana Area Council of Governments, to help inform the Regional Housing Study currently underway. Visit the Regional Dashboard for more context and draft findings from the study.

The planning team invites communitymembers to take part in this survey and contribute perspectives, experiences, insights, and ideas to the process.

The Michiana Area Council of Governments (MACOG) is a voluntary organization of local governments that studies and attempts to resolve areas of interlocal issues.
